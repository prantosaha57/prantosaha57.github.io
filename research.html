3D Reconstruction of Colonic Polyps: A Methodology for Improved Visualization and Analysis
Abstract

Colonoscopy remains the gold standard for colorectal cancer screening, yet conventional 2D imaging limits the surgeon's ability to assess polyp morphology and depth. This paper presents a methodology for 3D reconstruction of colonic polyps from standard colonoscopic video frames, enabling improved spatial visualization and analytical accuracy for clinical diagnosis.

Our approach integrates depth estimation, point cloud generation, and surface mesh reconstruction to produce high-fidelity 3D polyp models without requiring additional hardware. Experimental evaluations demonstrate significant improvement in polyp characterization compared to existing 2D approaches.

A Self-Supervised Hybrid Deep Learning Framework for Multi-Class Chest X-Ray Classification on Imbalanced Data
Abstract

Accurate automated classification of pulmonary diseases from chest X-rays is hampered by severe class imbalance in real-world clinical datasets. This study presents a hybrid deep learning framework combining self-supervised pre-training, advanced convolutional encoders, LSTM temporal modeling, and attention mechanisms to classify multiple pulmonary conditions from chest X-rays.

Our framework achieves 95.99% classification accuracy, outperforming existing state-of-the-art methods. The self-supervised pre-training strategy substantially reduces dependency on labeled data, making the approach viable for low-resource clinical settings. Class imbalance is addressed through a tailored data augmentation and loss weighting strategy.

Explainable AI for Breast Cancer Detection: Biglycan Biomarker Classification with Transfer Learning
Abstract

This paper presents an Explainable AI (XAI) framework for breast cancer detection using Biglycan as a biomarker. Biglycan, a small leucine-rich proteoglycan, has shown significant differential expression in cancerous versus healthy tissue. We leverage transfer learning to classify tissue samples and apply Grad-CAM and SHAP-based interpretability techniques to ensure clinical transparency.

The proposed approach demonstrates high diagnostic accuracy while providing radiologists with interpretable visual explanations of model decisions, supporting human-in-the-loop clinical workflows. This work bridges the gap between high-performance AI diagnostics and clinical trust.

Deep Learning for Automated Non-Crop Plant Seedling Detection: A CNN and Transfer Learning Approach for Precision Agriculture
Abstract

Weed management is a critical challenge in modern agriculture, with non-crop plant growth significantly reducing crop yields. This study presents a specialized CNN architecture for automated non-crop plant seedling detection, integrating advanced preprocessing pipelines, tailored convolutional layers, and optimized hyperparameters.

Achieving 99% classification accuracy across diverse plant species, our model outperforms general-purpose transfer learning baselines in this domain-specific task. The system offers a cost-effective, scalable solution for precision weed management, reducing reliance on manual labor and chemical herbicides.

Detecting Bengali Depressive Text: A Comparative Analysis of Machine Learning, Deep Learning, and Transformer Models
Abstract

Mental health disorders, particularly depression, are a growing public health concern in Bangladesh. Social media has emerged as a crucial platform for individuals to express mental states, yet NLP-based depression detection remains largely unexplored for the Bengali language. This research addresses this gap through a systematic comparative analysis of traditional ML, deep learning, and transformer-based approaches.

Using a curated Bengali social media dataset, our best-performing model — a GRU with GloVe embeddings — achieves 98.94% accuracy. We also evaluate BanglaBERT and other transformer architectures, providing insights into the tradeoff between model complexity and performance for low-resource NLP tasks. This work lays groundwork for AI-assisted mental health screening in Bengali-speaking communities.
