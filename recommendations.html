Letter of Recommendation — Dr. Md. Zahid Hasan
Associate Professor & Lab Director, Dept. of CSE · Daffodil International University

To Whom It May Concern,

It is my great pleasure to recommend Pranto Saha, whom I have had the privilege of supervising as both his undergraduate thesis supervisor and research mentor at the Health Informatics Research Lab (HIRL) since July 2022.

Pranto has consistently demonstrated exceptional research aptitude and intellectual curiosity. His work on self-supervised learning for chest X-ray classification has produced a framework achieving 95.99% accuracy — results that are publication-worthy and have been submitted to a leading Springer journal.

Beyond technical competence, Pranto shows remarkable independence, initiative, and collaborative spirit. He has made significant contributions to multiple research projects within our lab and has mentored junior researchers with patience and clarity.

I strongly recommend Pranto Saha for PhD admission without reservation. He has the intellectual foundation, work ethic, and research drive to thrive in a doctoral program.

Dr. Md. Zahid Hasan
Associate Professor & Lab Director, Dept. of CSE
Daffodil International University
zahid.cse@diu.edu.bd

Letter of Recommendation — Prof. Dr. Bimal Chandra Das
Associate Dean, FSIT · Dept. of ITM · Daffodil International University

To Whom It May Concern,

I write with great enthusiasm to recommend Pranto Saha for PhD candidacy. I have had the opportunity to observe Pranto's academic development over the course of his undergraduate studies at Daffodil International University.

Pranto is among the most diligent and academically gifted students I have encountered in my years in the faculty. His CGPA of 3.94/4.00 and multiple merit scholarships, including three instances of a perfect 4.00, speak to his consistent excellence.

I give my highest recommendation to Pranto Saha for doctoral study. I am confident he will excel and contribute meaningfully to his chosen research area.
