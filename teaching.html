Teaching Philosophy

I believe in adaptive, student-centered teaching. My goal is to bridge the gap between theory and practice — ensuring students not only understand concepts but can apply them to real-world problems. I place strong emphasis on clarity, engagement, and building problem-solving intuition.

Having taught at multiple levels — from lab sessions to full lecture courses — I understand the diverse challenges students face. I design my courses to be inclusive, practical, and enriched with hands-on projects.
